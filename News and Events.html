CLUB MEETING
DECEMBER 21
Join us for our upcoming car club meeting, where members will come together to discuss exciting plans for the year ahead. We'll cover upcoming drives, car shows, and community events, as well as share ideas for club projects and vehicle updates. Whether you're looking to showcase your ride, get advice on modifications, or simply connect with fellow enthusiasts, this meeting is the perfect opportunity to get involved. We look forward to seeing all members and welcoming new faces as we gear up for another exciting year of cars, camaraderie, and club activities!
CAR SHOW
January 2
Get ready for our upcoming car show, where car enthusiasts of all kinds will gather to celebrate incredible vehicles and automotive passion. The event will feature a wide variety of cars, from classic classics to modern marvels, along with competitions, awards, and plenty of photo opportunities. Whether you're showcasing your ride or just coming to admire the cars, this is an event you won't want to miss!
